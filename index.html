The end of the empty mile.

Why we exist

Right now, trucks are driving empty past loads that need moving. The market isn’t broken — it’s disconnected.

Foundations is the freight marketplace from Endurance Logistics. Shippers post loads. Carriers claim them in seconds. Pricing is transparent to both sides — like a ride-share network for freight, built for the people who actually move it. Deadhead falls, rates get honest, and everyone sees the same truth.

The System

One marketplace where every load finds the truck already heading its way.

01

Loads matched in seconds

Shippers post a load; the engine finds the right truck — vetted, nearby, and already pointed the right direction. No phone tag, no brokerage maze.

02

One market, both sides

Shippers and carriers see the same live network — the same loads, the same lanes, the same prices. Transparency isn’t a feature; it’s the market.

03

Live tracking on every load

From pickup to proof-of-delivery, both sides watch the same dot move on the same map. No check calls, no black holes.

04

Backhauls filled automatically

The moment a carrier drops a load, the engine is already filling the return leg. Empty miles become paid miles before the trailer doors close.

05

Rates without the black box

Market-rate pricing computed in the open and shown to both sides. Shippers stop overpaying; carriers stop getting squeezed.

06

A market that learns

Every matched load sharpens the network — better rates, tighter ETAs, smarter backhauls. The marketplace gets more efficient with every mile it fills.
