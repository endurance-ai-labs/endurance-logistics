Why we exist

Freight still moves on phone calls, clipboards, and guesswork. We think the physical world deserves software that acts.

Foundations is the AI-native TMS from Endurance Logistics. Not a dashboard bolted onto old workflows — an operating layer that sees, decides, and executes across your entire network.

The System

Imagine your operation as one intelligent network — every gate, dock, truck, and mile connected.

01

Agentic AI workflows, gate to dock

Autonomous agents check trucks in, assign doors, sequence moves, and close out loads — without a human dispatcher in the loop.

02

One pane of glass

Every facility, lane, and asset in a single live view. No more swivel-chairing between five systems to answer one question.

03

Computer vision at the edge

Cameras become sensors. The platform reads trailers, seals, and dock states in real time and turns sight into structured data.

04

Configurable to every site

Each yard and warehouse is different. The OS adapts to your layouts, rules, and SLAs — not the other way around.

05

Unlocks your existing stack

Your WMS and TMS hold value your team can't reach. We connect to them and put their data to work instead of replacing them.

06

Predictive, and improving daily

Every mission enriches the model. The network gets faster, cheaper, and more resilient with each load it moves.
